## What It Actually Is

It's an AI arena-style platform. You toss in a prompt, it fires back responses from multiple models side by side, and you get to pick your winner. Think of it as a cage match for LLMs. The lineup updates stupid fast too — new model drops on Tuesday, it's prob already on there by Wednesday. Gotta give 'em that.

## The Catch (Bc There's Always One)

- **Daily chat limit.** And no, it's not generous. You're not gonna be having marathon convos with Claude or GPT here. You get your handful of rounds and then you're done for the day. Touch grass or whatever.
- **They log EVERYTHING.** Your prompts, your convos, your weird 2am questions about whether a hotdog is a sandwich — all of it. Stored. Recorded. Collected.
- **"Academic Research" 🙄** — Here's the kicker. They straight up tell you they *might* share your conversations on their blog for so-called "academic research." Yeah ok buddy. Nothing says peer-reviewed science like publishing some rando's chat logs. Real scholarly stuff right there.

## The Verdict

Look, if you're broke and just wanna test drive a bunch of models without pulling out your credit card, it works. It does the job. But don't go pouring your heart out or dropping anything remotely sensitive in there unless you're cool with it potentially ending up as someone's blog content wrapped in an academic bow.

## Visual calm is a performance feature

When a page is visually noisy, readers spend attention before they spend time. Calm interfaces reduce friction.

## Three useful heuristics

1. Give content lanes a clear job.
2. Keep metadata compact and consistent.
3. Let motion support the atmosphere, not distract from it.

## Practical takeaway

A blog with fewer, stronger visual decisions usually feels more personal than one overloaded with novelty.

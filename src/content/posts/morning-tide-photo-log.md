## A gallery should still tell a story

The strongest image-led posts still benefit from sequence, pacing, and context. A gallery page becomes much more useful when it keeps the same metadata, taxonomy, and routing rules as every other post.

## Shared structure matters

By keeping gallery entries inside the same collection as essays and notes, the site stays easier to search, filter, and evolve.
